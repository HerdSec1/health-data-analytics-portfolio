# Domain 2: Business Needs Assessment

## Business Problem

Healthcare organizations rely on timely, accurate, and complete documentation to support patient care, coding, billing, compliance, and operational reporting.

When clinical documentation remains unsigned, incomplete, or delayed, it can create downstream issues such as:

- Delayed coding and billing workflows
- Reduced confidence in operational reporting
- Increased manual follow-up
- Incomplete information for review processes
- Potential documentation compliance concerns

## Business Question

How can clinical documentation status data be analyzed to identify patterns in unsigned, pending, or delayed notes?

## Stakeholders

Potential stakeholders for this analysis include:

- HIM leadership
- Coding teams
- Clinical department leadership
- Compliance or audit teams
- Revenue cycle leadership
- Data analytics teams

## Intended Outcome

The goal is to transform raw documentation activity into a structured summary that helps leadership understand:

- Which note types have the highest incomplete volume
- Which departments show delayed documentation patterns
- Whether documentation completion is improving or worsening
- Where follow-up or workflow review may be needed
